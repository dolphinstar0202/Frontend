## Usage

Please do not use the Tailwind name or logos in any way that could mistakenly imply any official connection with or endorsement of Tailwind Labs Inc. Any use of the Tailwind name or logos in a manner that could cause customer confusion is not permitted.

This includes naming a product or service in a way that emphasizes the Tailwind brand, like "Tailwind Themes" or "Tailwind Studio", as well as in domain names like "tailwindkits.com".

Instead, please use your own brand name in a way that clearly distinguishes it from Tailwind CSS.  Examples that are permitted include "ComponentStudio for Tailwind CSS" or "UtilityThemes: Templates for Tailwind CSS".

Additionally, please do not use our brand names or logos for t-shirts, stickers, or other merchandise without explicit written consent.
